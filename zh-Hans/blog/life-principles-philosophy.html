人生信条 (Life Principles Philosophy)

2025年1月28日 · 阅读需 2 分钟
Guangze Yang
Master of Control System, Ibaraki University

我的人生信条​

牢记两句古训：

  1. 己所不欲, 勿施于人 - Don't impose on others what you don't want for yourself
  2. 以德报怨, 何以报德 - If you repay evil with kindness, how will you repay kindness?

自我修养 (Self-Cultivation)​

  • 尊重他人：不要强迫别人做自己不愿意做的事
  • 明智待人：善意要给予值得的人, 而非那些恶意相向者

做真实的自己 (Being Authentic)​

  • 无需盲从他人 - No need to blindly follow others
  • 无需讨好所有人 - No need to please everyone
  • 无需对所有人都友善 - No need to be kind to everyone
  • 可以有任何情绪 - It's okay to have any emotions

成长 (Growth)​

  • 专注自我提升 - Focus on self-improvement
  • 不必在意他人评价 - Don't worry about others' opinions
  • 循序渐进地成长 - Grow step by step
  • 坚定自我价值 - Stand firm in your self-worth
  • 无条件相信自己 - Believe in yourself unconditionally

面对烦恼 (Dealing with Troubles)​

如果一件事情烦恼你，就想去解决它，而如果解决不了，那就做出计划想办法解决它。

如果一个人烦恼你，就想办法远离他，而如果远离不了，那就无视他。

人生态度 (Life Attitude)​

我为爱我的人而存在。

就算人生不是很精彩，我也要勇敢的姿态。

Key Philosophy Points​

Ancient Wisdom Applied:

  • Practice reciprocity in relationships
  • Be discerning with kindness - give it to those who deserve it

Authentic Living:

  • Stay true to yourself without seeking universal approval
  • Embrace your emotions and responses naturally

Growth Mindset:

  • Focus inward on self-improvement rather than external validation
  • Build unshakeable self-confidence through consistent action

Problem-Solving Approach:

  • For troublesome situations: solve them or make a plan to solve them
  • For troublesome people: distance yourself or ignore them

A personal manifesto for living authentically while maintaining wisdom in relationships and personal development.
